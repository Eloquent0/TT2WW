About TT2WW

TT2WW (Text To Wave Words) converts speech dynamics into expressive typography. It maps measured audio intensity to font size and color at the word level.

What It Does

  • Accepts audio/video uploads up to 10 minutes
  • Generates or accepts transcript text
  • Aligns words to timing and decibel data
  • Renders animated, data-driven typography output

How It Works

The app builds a dB timeline from audio, splits text into word rows, and assigns each word a mean/max volume profile. Mapping modes then transform dB to visual size curves.

Why It Exists

TT2WW explores the relationship between sound and visual language—making spoken rhythm and emphasis visible for creative storytelling and analysis.

Output Options

  • Copy rich styled output
  • Export word-level CSV metadata
  • Play animated read-through with waveform scrubber
